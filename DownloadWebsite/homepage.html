About VoteCoin


since 2023

Jaivon Harris, Daniel Jeong, and Nicholas Vroman are on a mission to revolutionize the democratic process with their groundbreaking capstone project, VoteCoin. This visionary team of three has embarked on an ambitious endeavor to create a robust and secure voting system that leverages the power of blockchain technology. VoteCoin is set to be a game-changer in the world of elections, bringing transparency, security, and accessibility to the forefront. By harnessing the immutable and decentralized nature of blockchain, Jaivon, Daniel, and Nicholas aim to eliminate the vulnerabilities and doubts that have plagued traditional voting systems.Their project will not only ensure the integrity of the electoral process but also enable citizens to cast their votes conveniently and remotely. With VoteCoin, the team aspires to usher in a new era of trust and confidence in the electoral system, promising to redefine democracy for generations to come. Join them on their journey as they make history and empower individuals to participate in the future of governance.
